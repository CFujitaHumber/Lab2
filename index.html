What Are Apples

The Amazing Fruit

Apples are a fruit. Fruit comes from a plant after pollination, and have seeds in them to grow. Apples are a well-known common fruit, and people eat them every day all over the world. Apples are sweet and sometimes sour, which makes them a fantastic ingredient in cooking and baking.
